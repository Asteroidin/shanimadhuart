Digital Art Mastery

A comprehensive guide to digital painting, from concepts to final render.

Beginner Level $99

Taught by Alex Roe

Professional Digital Artist

Dive deep into the world of digital art with this comprehensive course. You'll learn the fundamentals of digital painting, from setting up your canvas and brushes to understanding color theory, lighting, and composition. By the end of this course, you will have the skills to create stunning and professional-level digital illustrations.

  • Over 10 hours of video content
  • Downloadable resources and brushes
  • Access to a private student community
  • Module 1: Getting Started with Digital Tools
  • Module 2: The Art of Line and Form
  • Module 3: Advanced Color Theory and Lighting

Alex Roe is a renowned digital artist with over 15 years of experience in the industry. Her work has been featured in major publications and art galleries worldwide. She is passionate about teaching and sharing her knowledge with aspiring artists.
